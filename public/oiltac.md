# OILTAC Product Overview
Last updated: 2026

## Short Summary
OILTAC is a diesel and petroleum fuel additive designed to improve combustion efficiency, reduce visible smoke and deposits, and lower fuel consumption in diesel engines, marine vessels, generators, boilers, and other fuel-burning equipment.

OILTAC is manufactured by Fukasawa Chemical Laboratory Inc. in Japan and distributed in North America by Chie Tsiang Enterprise.

## Who OILTAC Is For
OILTAC is intended for operators of equipment that burns diesel, heavy fuel oil, bunker fuel, A-type fuel oil, or other liquid petroleum fuels.

Common users include:
- Tugboat and marine vessel operators
- Port and harbor vessel fleets
- Diesel generator operators
- Industrial boiler and furnace operators
- Rail and locomotive operators
- Mining and heavy equipment operators

## What Problem OILTAC Solves
OILTAC is used when fuel-burning equipment has problems such as:
- High fuel consumption
- Visible smoke or soot
- Carbon buildup
- Fuel system fouling
- Injector and exhaust deposits
- Bunker or fuel tank sludge
- Maintenance caused by incomplete combustion

## How OILTAC Is Used
OILTAC is added directly to fuel at a standard volumetric ratio of 1:10,000.

One liter of OILTAC treats 10,000 liters of fuel.

No engine modification, dosing hardware, or special injection system is required.

## Main Benefits
Reported benefits from laboratory and field testing include:
- Lower fuel consumption
- Reduced smoke and soot
- Cleaner combustion
- Reduced carbon deposits
- Cleaner fuel, combustion, and exhaust systems
- Reduced sludge formation in heavy fuel oil storage and bunkering systems
- Improved exhaust gas composition

## Evidence Summary
OILTAC has been evaluated in independent and field tests involving marine diesel engines, locomotives, road vehicles, diesel generator sets, boilers, and fuel-burning industrial equipment.

Reported fuel consumption reductions vary by engine type, fuel type, load, and operating conditions. Documented tests include results in the approximate range of 1 percent to 7 percent fuel savings, with several long-term field applications reporting sustained reductions of several percent.

Testing has also reported reductions in smoke, soot, carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, nitrogen oxides, sulfur dioxide, deposits, and fuel system fouling.

For detailed test data, see:
- [OILTAC Technical Reference](./oiltac-technical-reference.md)

## Important Caveat
One 200-hour marine diesel test using an A/C heavy oil blend documented increased piston ring wear while also showing reduced crank pin bearing wear and improved deposit characteristics. The test authors recommended further testing for heavy oil blend applications.

This result should be considered when evaluating OILTAC for heavy oil or bunker fuel operations. No similar piston ring wear issue is reported in the broader standard diesel and light oil test record.

## Pilot Program
OILTAC is available through a 45 to 60 day pilot program using the operator's own equipment and fuel records.

Pilot details:
- Standard dose: 1 liter per 10,000 liters of fuel
- Pilot rate: $45 per liter
- Typical pilot kit: 10 liters
- Pilot kit price: $450
- Fuel treated by 10 liter kit: 100,000 liters

The pilot is designed to compare fuel consumption and operating conditions before and after OILTAC use using the operator's existing logs.
